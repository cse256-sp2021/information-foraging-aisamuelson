Application Process

Before You Apply:

Parents interested in Washington University Nursery School should contact the school to schedule a visit.

Applying to Washington University Nursery School:

An application fee of $40 is required when applying to the nursery school. After this is received, your child is placed on an enrollment list. You will be notified of acceptance in the March preceding the fall enrollment date.
