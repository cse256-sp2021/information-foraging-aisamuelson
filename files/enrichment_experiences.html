Enrichment Experiences

The Washington University Nursery School offers extra activities to compliment the classroom experiences. These include enrichment programs in art, music, Spanish, and science which are included within the Nursery School curriculum; Bear Tracks, and extended day program; and Summer Camp which is offered after normal school programming concluded for the year.
